Meet Dr. Tanupa Patel

Dr. Tanupa, MD (Internal Medicine), is a distinguished practitioner with a remarkable journey in holistic healthcare. Armed with a Bachelor's in Homoeopathy Medicine and Surgery, Dr. Tanupa's passion for healing led her to pursue advanced qualifications. With a commitment to excellence, Dr. Tanupa further honed her skills through a Post Graduate Diploma in Emergency Services and a Fellowship in Community Paediatrics. Her diverse expertise allows her to address a wide range of health concerns with a holistic approach.
